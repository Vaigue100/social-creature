Achievements Guide

What are Achievements?

Achievements are milestones you unlock as you collect more chatlings. Each achievement rewards you with points!

How to Unlock Achievements

  • Collect chatlings from your liked YouTube videos
  • Reach collection milestones (10, 50, 100+ chatlings)
  • Find rare and legendary chatlings
  • Complete special challenges

Achievement Points

Each achievement is worth a certain number of points. Your total points are displayed at the top of this page.

Progress Tracking

For collection-based achievements, you'll see a progress bar showing how many more chatlings you need to collect. Keep liking videos and claiming rewards to unlock more achievements!
